How to make Bronze

Four Bronze mixes is made from three Copper dusts and one Tin dust.

To make Copper and Tin dust. Place one Ingot along with the Wooden Grader in the Crafting Gride. 




How to make Steel

Steel is made in the Railcraft Blast Furnace Multiblock. To form the Multiblock, place Blast Furnace Bricks in a 3 by 3, and 4 tall. With a hollow center.

You then cook Iron with Coal Coke, or Charcoal.
