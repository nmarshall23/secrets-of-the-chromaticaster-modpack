§2Getting Started Guide

§4Beginning Tools

You should have spawned with a set of wooden tools. Those tools will not last you very long. You are going to want to find some gravel, to make some flint tools. You can craft three gravel into one flint.

Also the flint Axe can chop down a tree. Hold shift to break logs normally.


§4Food and Farming

Those Apples are not going to last very long. You are going to have to break some grass to find seeds. Also you will need to make a flint hoe to make farmland.


§4Furnace

Cook up those Apples. After you have Potatos Cook those up too.
